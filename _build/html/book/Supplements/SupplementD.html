Supplement D: Similar NALT terms in different hierarchies

Supplement D: Similar NALT terms in different hierarchies#

Similar or seemingly-the-same words or phrases can have very different meanings and hierarchies. Review definitions, BTs, NTs, RTs, definitions, scope notes and entry-level terms. If needed, check the full-text article to ensure that you are selecting the appropriate concept(s).

Some examples are listed below:

a. absorption versus adsorption

b. agricultural land versus agricultural landscapes

c. Alegria (preferred term Luehea) versus Alegria (not in NALT, place name in Brazil, Philippines, Spain, El Salvador and Turkey or a Mexican snack food, among other possible definitions)

d. Amazona versus Amazonia

e. ammonia versus Ammonia (Rotaliidae)

f. Bacillus (bacteria) versus Bacillus (Orthoptera)

g. binding proteins versus protein binding

h. Carnivora versus carnivores versus carnivorous fungi (preferred term predatory fungi) versus carnivorous plants

i. catechin versus catechins (preferred term flavanols)

j. chemical constituents of plants versus phytochemicals

k. China versus Republic of China (preferred term Taiwan)

l. choroid (not a NALT term, MESH BT eyes -> uvea) versus choroid plexus, BT nervous system -> plexus)

m. coriander (preferred term Coriandrum sativum) versus coriander leaves (preferred term cilantro) versus coriander seed

n. crop (digestive system) versus [crops] (https://lod.nal.usda.gov/nalt/7140)

o. crystalline (not a NALT term, definition – “composed of crystals”) versus crystallins versus crystallites

p. dialysis versus hemodialysis

q. digestibility versus digestion

r. Dolichoderus (not a NALT term, is genus under Formicidae) versus Dolichodorus

s. drought versus drought stress (preferred term water stress) versus drought tolerance versus drought tolerant plants

t. ecology versus ecosystems

u. electric current versus electric power versus electricity

v. Equatorial Guinea versus Guinea versus Guinea-Bissau

w. Erosion (Poaceae) (preferred term Eragrostis) versus erosion (appears in several NALT terms, e.g., erosion control, genetic erosion, soil erosion, tooth erosion, Water Erosion Prediction Project, wind erosion)

x. fish versus FISH (preferred NALT concept fluorescence in situ hybridization)

y. energy intake versus feed intake versus food intake versus nutrient intake

z. genetic resistance vs resistance genes

aa. geographic coordinate systems versus geographic information systems versus global positioning system

bb. Georgia versus Republic of Georgia

cc. greenhouse gas emissions versus greenhouse gas inventory (preferred term carbon footprint) versus greenhouse gases

dd. Hawaii (entire state of Hawaii) versus Hawaii (island) (individual island of Hawaii)

ee. hippocampus versus Hippocampus (Syngnathidae)

ff. honeydew versus honeydew honey versus honeydew melons

gg. infants versus children

hh. Insectivora versus insectivores

ii. lactate (preferred term lactic acid) versus lactates

jj. Lens versus lens (preferred term eye lens)

kk. Macadamia versus macadamia nuts

ll. Mediterranean islands versus Mediterranean region versus Mediterranean Sea

mm. Nitrospira (Class) versus Nitrospira (genus)

nn. New Guinea versus Papua New Guinea (BT New Guinea)

oo. olefin versus olefins (preferred term alkenes)

pp. Otolithes versus otoliths

qq. pepper, BT spices, RT Piper versus peppers BT vegetables, RT Capsicum

rr. planting, (place seeds or seedlings directly into ground) versus sowing, (scattering seeds over the ground)

ss. Platypus versus platypuses (preferred term Ornithorhynchus anatinus)

tt. repeatability versus [reproducibility] (https://lod.nal.usda.gov/nalt/52491)

uu. satellite DNA versus satellites

vv. Sclerotium (genus) versus sclerotium (fungal anatomy) (preferred term sclerotia)

ww. Sepsis (Diptera) versus sepsis (infection)

xx. soil fertility versus soil quality

yy. soil maps versus soil surveys

zz. Stella (not a NALT term, is a sweet cherry varietal name) versus Stella

aaa. Togo (Africa) versus Togo (Heteroptera)

bbb. Turkey (country), (new accepted name Türkiye) versus turkeys
